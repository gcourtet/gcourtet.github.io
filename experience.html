Referee for the French Basketball Federation (2010-2013) Morbihan, France

I was only 15 years old when I started being a referee for the French Basketball Federation (FFBB) and I was officiating on games where the players where sometimes 20 years older than me... Imagine that : a kid trying to tell you wether you do things correctly or not and having more authority than you on the court. Some players had trouble understanding that and that enabled to learn how to be respected when it is needed but also how to talk to other people and try to covince them and to stay very calm and respectful while doing it. Once again it was a very genuine experience and I have learned a lot on myself during the 3 years I've spent officiating.
