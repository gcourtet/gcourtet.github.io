Intern at METI Software & Services (2015) Larmor-Plage, France

During my month with METI, I've had the opportunity to disover the evolution of a project inside a company from scratch to a complete product. I was navigating from one service to another in order to really get an idea of what it is like to work on a project inside a company. Summer job at E.Leclerc (Summer 2015 & 2016) Lanester, France

Being a student is great but if you want to really enjoy all the aspects of student life, you have to earn some money and to stop being completely dependent on your parents. That was the purpose of my two summers at E.Leclerc : earn some money. However I was surprised to also learn a lot of things especially on how difficult it can be to work in a team sometimes but also to manage a team. Referee for the French Basketball Federation (2010-2013) Morbihan, France

I was only 15 years old when I started being a referee for the French Basketball Federation (FFBB) and I was officiating on games in which the players were sometimes 20 years older than me... Imagine that : a kid trying to tell you wether or not you're doing things correctly and having more authority than you on the court. Some players had trouble understanding that and that enabled me to learn how to be respected when it is needed but also how to talk to other people and try to covince them with calm and respect. Once again it was a very genuine experience and I have learned a lot on myself during the 3 years I've spent officiating.
